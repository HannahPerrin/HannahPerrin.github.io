Covid-19 Update

Our New Policies and Procedures

- Face masks must been worn for the duration of your appointment when possible. If you don't have your own, we will provide one for a small cost.

- Appointments are limited to ensure we keep to the distancing guidelines. Please come alone and on time.

- Please let us know if you are unable to make your appointment.

- Clients will be seen by appointment only. Please book appointments over the phone.

- If you feel unwell, please stay at home.

- We will no longer be serving refreshments, to stop cross contamination.

- All surfaces and equipment will be cleaned after every client.

- Please wash or sanitise hands on arrival.

- We will be working new hours and will be offering limited services.

- Some services have had a price increase taking effect from July 1st.

- Please use card payments when possible.

About Us

Headquarters Hair Salon is one of Schwarzkopf's premier salons in the North West, located in Parbold. We have 3 senior stylists, 1 junior stylist, 1 apprentice and 1 beauty therapist, who has over 14 years experience. We cover gents, children, ladies, colour, perms/straightening & hair extensions, and offer a wide range of beauty services, including; semi permanent eyelashes, waxing, tinting, CDN Shellac & Nouveau LVL.

Headquarters has recently been taken under new management, and in July 2018, the salon was relaunched, bringing with it an entirely new look and feel.
